About

This is an HDR photo gallery of US National Parks and other inspiring places. HDR photography is a technique that reveals hidden details by merging several photos taken at different exposures.

This project was made possible by the support of my traveling partner, Hilde. I will update the site whenever I get new material ready and find internet connection. Thanks for checking it out!
